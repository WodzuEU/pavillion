Project: The Pavilion

Location: Fictional Site, Earth

Architect: Elemental Architecture

Year: 2024

The Pavilion is a study in minimalism and the interplay between structure and nature. Designed to be a place of quiet contemplation, its form is derived from the stark, elemental shapes found in the surrounding landscape.
